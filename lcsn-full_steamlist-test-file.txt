Notice: Ver. 1.101.1 Known Issues and Hotfix ETA
Hello, this is Project Moon.

We would like to inform you about the known issues identified after the Mar. 26th, 2026 Scheduled Update, and the ETA for a Hotfix that addresses some of them.

Please continue reading for more information.

[ Issues to be fixed with the Mar. 27th Hotfix ]

▶ An Issue where, under specific circumstances, completion of certain 'Mirror Projection / Achievement - Loadout' Achievements are abnormally affected

※ Affected Achievements (9 total)
1. Defeat the 4 'Charm, Wander, Doubt,' 'Textbook,' 'Blade and Artwork,' and 'The Unsevering' Theme Pack bosses with 3 or more The House of Spiders Identities, with 'LCB Sinner Ryōshū', in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
2. Defeat the 'The Heartbreaking' Theme Pack boss with 3 or more Wuthering Heights Identities, including 'Wild Hunt Heathcliff', in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
3. Defeat the 'The Infinite Procession' Theme Pack boss with 'Cinq West Section 3 Meursault,' 'Zwei West Section 3 Sinclair,' 'Fanghunt Office Fixer Hong Lu,' and 'Firefist Office Survivor Gregor' Identities in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
4. Defeat the 'Murder on the WARP Express BokGak' Theme Pack boss with 4 or more Bloodfiend Identities in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
5. Defeat the 'The Unconfronting' Theme Pack boss with 2 or more Mechanical Amalgam Identities in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
6. Defeat the 'Miracle in District 20 BokGak' Theme Pack boss with only Heathcliff and Don Quixote Identities on the field (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
7. Defeat the 'Timekilling Time BokGak' Theme Pack boss with 2 or more T Corp. Identities in your Loadout (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
8. Defeat the 'A Beautiful Voice' Theme Pack boss with 'Wild Hunt Heathcliff' Identity on the field (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)
9. Defeat the 5 'Murder on the WARP Express,' 'Murder on the WARP Express BokGak,' 'La Manchaland Re-opening,' 'The Infinite Procession,' and 'The Dream Ending' Theme Pack bosses with 'The Manager of La Manchaland Don Quixote' Identity on the field (Not counting Backup Units; incompletable by Rental Teams)

( Issue Details )
- This issue can be caused by forfeiting immediately after entering the Boss Encounter, then re-entering with a different Team loadout
1. Even after re-entering with a different loadout, because the game reads the previous Team loadout instead, Achievements that should be (or shouldn't be) completable with the current loadout are not completed (or completed)
2. Loadout Achievements with "Not counting Backup Units" condition count Backup Units for the purpose of completing them

( Issue Cause )
- When re-entering an Encounter after forfeiting it, the in-game data used for Achievement tracking does not properly update with the user's current Team loadout, causing this issue to occur.

( Issue Solution )
- Additional code was inserted to update the in-game data used for Achievement tracking. This solution will be deployed via a hotfix.


[ Hotfix ETA ]
- 3.27(Fri) 16:00(KST)
- There may be a 10 minute delay.

※ The Hotfix will be deployed upon restarting the game, and does not require a separate client update. However, please note that additional in-game downloads may occur.


[ Issues to be resolved with a Scheduled Update ]

▶ An issue where certain Encounters are missing BGM and connected lyrics

▶ An issue where the Mirror Dungeon EXTREME Theme Pack 'Bridle of Infinity' enemy unit's "Surgery" Keyword does not work

※ The above issues will be resolved with the April 2nd Scheduled Update.


We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience.

Notice: The 8th Walpurgis Night Mission Issue Fixed
Hello, this is Project Moon.

Following the Mar. 19th, 2026 Scheduled Update, we identified an issue in the 8th Walpurgis Night where a certain mission was not being counted properly, preventing players from claiming rewards.

This issue has now been resolved and deployed to the server.
Please refer to the details below for more information.

[ Issue Details ]
- Following the Mar. 19th Scheduled Update, a mission under the "Hard" Difficulty in the 8th Walpurgis Night mission list was not counted properly, making it impossible to obtain its reward.
※ Affected mission: Complete all Missions in both Easy and Hard Difficulties

[ Cause of the Issue ]
- The mission's end date was incorrectly set during validation process, preventing mission progress even when all conditions were met.

[ Fix Details ]
- Corrected the valid period for the mission "Complete all Missions in both Easy and Hard Difficulties"
- Improved and deployed server logic to ensure mission progress is properly reflected

[ Fix Schedule ]
- Fix applied on Mar. 20th (Fri), 21:50 (KST)

※ The fix has been automatically applied as of Mar. 20th, 21:50. No additional update is required.

※ After the time listed above, mission progress will now be properly counted. We have confirmed that rewards can now be claimed.
For Managers who were unable to complete the mission due to this issue, please fully restart the game and reconnect.

We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience.
